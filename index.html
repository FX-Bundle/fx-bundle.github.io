Eight fully parametric bands with adjustable frequency, gain, and Q. Wide ±18 dB range, high/low-pass filters, and 2x oversampled engine. Seamless control for pristine sound in any workflow.

Multi-Band Dynamics Mastery

How It Works

Step 1: Install and Activate

Download the bundle. It's pre-activated—no keys or purchases needed. Drag plugins into your DAW's plugin folder.

Step 2: Build Your Chain

Insert plugins on tracks or buses. EQ for shaping, ThrillMe or NightShine for dynamics, Samurai Delay or Schroeder for space, visualizers for analysis.

Step 3: Dial In Precision

Use Band Joiner for flexible multiband setups. Adjust parameters with real-time metering and spectrograms. A/B presets from the expanded library.

Step 4: Export Pristine Audio

Low-latency engines ensure clean renders. Oversampling minimizes artifacts. Ready for streaming, vinyl, or any format.
